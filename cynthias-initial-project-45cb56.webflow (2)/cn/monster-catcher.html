Monster Catcher

UX / UI  •   Illustration   •   Concept

This is an augmented reality kids’ gaming app. Monster Catcher could help kids get over their fear of monsters and go to sleep independently. Monster Catcher can also provide a chance for parents to play with their kids. Children can use the camera to look around their bedroom in the Monster Catcher app. Eventually, they will find a monster somewhere, such as under the bed. Then kids could interact with this monster, drag it to the right monster’s planet, then this room will be clean again, no monsters anymore. Finally, the kid will have a good night sleep without worrying about a monster hidden under their bed.

1. Idea

1.1 Why kids need the Monster Catcher?

Bedtime should be a special and memorable part of a little one’s day. However, for some kids, when it’s time to turn off the light and say goodnight, fear and anxiety set in, turning the bedtime routine into a bedtime battle that’s a stressful experience for the entire family. Some children mention they are afraid to sleep because they're afraid of monsters. They believe there are monsters inside the room. At night, they can’t fall in sleep without parents and light. Even after they fall asleep, they could have a nightmare. A child’s sleep problem also make parents crazy, especially after a long day of work.
